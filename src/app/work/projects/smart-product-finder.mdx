## **Product Category Finder**  
### **Simplifying Product Setup for Shopify Store Owners**  

### **Overview**  
The Product Category Finder was an early AI-powered feature at Shopify aimed at streamlining product setup for store owners. Before this solution, merchants had to manually navigate an extensive list of product categories, which was time-consuming and prone to errors. By leveraging machine learning, the new system automatically suggested relevant product categories based on product images, titles, and descriptions, making the setup process faster and more intuitive.  

### **Key Features**  
- **AI-Powered Category Suggestions**: The system analyzed product images, titles, and descriptions to recommend the most relevant product categories.  
- **Default Category Selection**: Suggested categories were pre-selected, reducing the need for manual input.  
- **Improved User Experience**: A clean, intuitive interface allowed store owners to review and confirm AI-generated recommendations effortlessly.  
- **Accuracy & Precision**: The model was fine-tuned to minimize errors and ensure suggested categories aligned with user expectations.  

![Smart Product Finder](/images/projects/product-finder/smart-product-finder-flow.png)

### **Challenges & Learnings**  
- **Manual Selection Was a Bottleneck**: Store owners previously had to search for the correct category, leading to frustration and inefficiency.  
- **Balancing AI Automation with User Trust**: Ensuring users felt confident in AI-generated recommendations required clear UX design and safeguards.  
- **Avoiding Business Impact Risks**: Misclassified categories could affect store organization, tax settings, and search visibility, making accuracy a top priority.  
- **Iterative AI Development**: As an early AI-powered feature, careful testing and refinement were necessary to prevent misclassification issues.  

### **Outcomes**  
- **Faster Product Setup**: Store owners spent less time categorizing products, allowing them to focus on other aspects of their business.  
- **Reduced Errors**: AI-driven recommendations improved accuracy, minimizing misclassifications.  
- **Foundation for Future AI Advancements**: This project contributed to the early stages of AI at Shopify, ultimately leading to the development of Shopify Magic.
